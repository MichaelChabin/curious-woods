## Geometry Workspace Activities (on the coordinate plane)

- Multiplication as area — working prototype exists. See [[20-SPECS/Spec Multiplication Explorer]]
- Coordinate plane construction — child builds it through Euclidean gestures. See [[10-PROJECTS/Geometry/Geometry Workspace Overview]]
- Stained glass patterns — compass-and-straightedge art. See [[10-PROJECTS/Geometry/Geometry Constructions/Geometry 1st Constructions - Stained Glass]]
- Polygon construction — hexagons, squares, pentagons, semi-regular tilings. See [[10-PROJECTS/Geometry/Geometry Constructions/Geometry 2 Polygons]]
- Cross-cultural geometry panels — Islamic, Chinese, Japanese, Indian, Mesoamerican pattern traditions. See [[10-PROJECTS/Geometry/Geometry Constructions/Geometry 3 Latices and Recursion]]
- Golden rectangle and recursive growth
- Traversal activities — graph theory, Euler paths, mazes, random walks, grid-path counting
- Tables and grids — multiplication tables, number grids, counting puzzles
- Curves — function plotting, Bézier curves
- Construction history and replay — stepping through construction sequence as proof
- Transformations — reflection, rotation, scaling as canvas operations
- Trigonometry via unit circle — point moving around unit circle traces sine and cosine
- Area under curves by counting grid squares (Riemann sums)
- Linear equations — two points define a line, slope is visible, systems solved by intersection
